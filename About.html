Introduction Summary

As technology grows over the years, it's no surprise that the technology we use for transport and autonomous vehicles is also developing, we have electric cars slowly but steady becoming the norm, drones in the air being used for all sorts of stuff: from public to business and underwater vehicles that survey the land. There are also many factors around autonomous vehicles: the fact that there are so many levels of autonomy, the research from the past and what we have already discovered, what we are implementing right now in this day and age. However with good also comes with cons, foreseen problems often include how 'are these machines ethical?', the social impact of the vehicles and the challenges we face when combating the difficult questions. Finally, what does the future hold? Will we get all the solutions we want and with the use of these self-driving vehicles, will our world become an Utopia?
